Londrina's Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website will work as the Chamber of Commerce for the city of Londrina - Parana / Brazil. It will hold events, information, and contact of important people in town to allow everybody to create a good networking for future businesses.

Available Domain

londrinacommerce.com

Target Audience

People who want to know what is happening in Londrina such as: Events, Networking Opportunities, sponsors for the Agriculture Fair, Information About the City, etc.

Personas

Occupation: Local Business Owner

She owns a new alternative clothes store. She wants to check the different fairs that happen in town and how to put a stand for her store on those events.

Occupation: Local Farmer

He wants to sponsor the Local Agriculture Fair (the biggest in all South America), for that he needs to check the people responsible for it and the available spots for sponsorship.

Scenarios:

  1. What is the event calendar for the city?
  2. Information about regional events
  3. List of names to contact about comum local business
  4. Community events and public places available
  5. Charity and Opportunities to serve the Community
